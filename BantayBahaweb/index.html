Introducing BantayBaha PH

Your essential companion for flood safety and awareness in the Philippines.

The app provides real-time flood level monitoring, allowing you to track conditions as they change. In critical situations, you can instantly request a rescue, ensuring help is just a tap away. You can also mark yourself as safe, offering peace of mind to your loved ones. Additionally, the app sends important notifications and alerts about flood warnings, weather updates, and evacuation advisories, helping you stay ahead of any potential danger. With BantayBaha PH, you have the power to take control of your safety and respond effectively to flood emergencies.
